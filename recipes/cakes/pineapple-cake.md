---
name: PINEAPPLE CAKE
source: Cornell Cookbook
---

5oz butter
5oz sugar
2 beaten eggs
5.5oz flour
2oz custard powder
1/2 t baking powder
1 c sultanas
1/2 small tin crushed pineapple

---

Cream butter and sugar.  Add beaten egg.  Mix in dry ingredients and fruit.

Bake 180C for 45 minutes in 7 inch square tin.
