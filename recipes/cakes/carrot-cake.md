---
name: CARROT CAKE 1
---

1 c wholemeal flour
1 c flour
2 t baking soda
2 c raw sugar
1 c cooking oil
4 eggs, beaten
2 c finely grated carrot
2 t cinnamon

---

Sift dry ingredients, add oil, stir in beaten eggs and carrot.  Mix thoroughly.  Pour into a 9 inch greased tin.  Bake at 190C for 1 hour.
