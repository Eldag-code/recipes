---
name: Russian Square
source: Rally cook book, page 72
cookTime: '20'
preparationTime: '0'
---
6oz butter
1 c sugar
2 D  golden syrup
2 eggs
2 c sultanas
2 c flour
2 t baking powder
1 t vanilla essence
---
Melt butter, sugar and syrup.  Cool.  Add egg and other ingredients.   Bake at 180C for 20 minutes.
