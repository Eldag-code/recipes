---
name: Nutty Fingers
source: Onslow College Cookbook, page 143
cookTime: '20'
---

1 c coconut
3/4 c brown sugar
1/2 c walnuts, chopped
4 oz butter
1 c rolled oats
1 t baking powder
pinch of salt

---

Melt butter and add to dry ingredients.  Mix well.  Press into sponge roll tin and bake at 140C for 20 minutes.
