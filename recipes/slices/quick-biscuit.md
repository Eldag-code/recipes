---
name: Quick Biscuit
source: Cornell Cookbook
---

5oz butter
1T golden syrup
8oz flour
1 c sugar
1 c cornflakes
1/2 c peanuts
1t baking powder
1 egg, beaten

---

Melt butter and sugar.  Add remaining ingredients and mix well.  Bake in a Swiss roll tin at 150C for 20 minutes.  When cool ice with lemon icing.
