---
name: honey loaf
source: Rally cook book, page 29
cookTime: '60'
preparationTime: '15'
comments: OK
---

8oz flour
4oz sugar
1 t baking powder
1 t baking soda
300ml water, boiling
2 T honey

---

Mix flour, sugar and baking powder.  Add the boiling water with honey and soda dissolved in it.  Pour into greased tin and bake at 180C for 1 hour.
