---
name: Carrot Plum Pudding
source: Cornell Cookbook
---

1 c flour
4oz sugar
1t cinnamon
1t mixed spice
pinch salt
1 grated carrot
1 c mixed fruit
1T butter
1t baking soda
3/4 c milk

---

Put all dry ingredients except for the baking soda in bowl and add fruit and carrot.  Add butter and soda dissolved in warm milk.  Steam for 3 hours
