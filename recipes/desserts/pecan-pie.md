---
name: Pecan pie
source: Well done cooking classes
---
3 eggs
180g water
420g brown sugar
1/2 t salt
60g butter, melted
1 T brandy/rum
2 t vanilla
240g pecan pieces
---
Mix together.  Fill pie crusts and bake at 400 F for 10 minutes, then reduce temp to 325 F and bake until center is set, about 65 minutes.
