---
name: Cornflake Cookies
source: Notebook
---
125g sugar
125g butter
1 egg
175g flour
1t baking powder
1/2 c raisins
pinch salt
1 c cornflakes
---
Cream butter and sugar.  Add lightly beaten egg.  Sift in flour and baking powder.  Mix in raisins, salt and cornflakes.  Bake at 180C for 20 minutes.
