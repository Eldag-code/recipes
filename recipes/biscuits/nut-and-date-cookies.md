---
name: nut and date cookies
source: Rally cook book, page 17
cookTime: '20'
preparationTime: '0'
---
6oz butter
8oz brown sugar
2 eggs, well beaten
1 lb flour
1 t salt
1 t baking soda
Vanilla
1 c dates, chopped
1 c peanuts
---
Cream butter and sugar, add eggs then dry ingredients and fruit.  Put in small balls on tray and flatten them.  Bake at 180C for 15-20 minutes.
