---
name: Brownies
source: Cornell Cookbook
---
1 1/2 cups sugar
90gm butter or margarine 
3 eggs
1 c flour
1/2 c cocoa
1t baking powder
1/2t vanilla
3/4 c chopped nuts
---
Cream sugar and butter.  Add beaten eggs and mix well.  Sift in remaining dry ingredients then mix in vanilla and chopped nuts.  Cook at 180C for 15 minutes.
