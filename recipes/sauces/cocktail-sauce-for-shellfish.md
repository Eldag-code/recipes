---
name: cocktail sauce for shellfish
source: Rally cook book, page 9
cookTime: '0'
preparationTime: '0'
---
3-4 T mayonnaise
2 T tomato sauce
Dash of Worcester sauce
Salt
Pepper
Lemon juice
1/4 pt whipped cream
---
Mix ingredients and lightly add into whipped cream.  Chill.  Top shellfish with sauce.
